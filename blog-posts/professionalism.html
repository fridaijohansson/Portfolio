Professionalism within creative industries

Thursday, 29 September 2022

by Frida Johansson

What is professionalism?

Professionalism is the ability to contribute to a working environment respectfully and responsibly. Opposing prejudice towards people and backgrounds by engaging in a superficial connection in which the primary focus is to produce the expected work. It is about being truthful of your ability to do the work and follow through on your obligations. Along with this, it also means you should follow the codes of conduct within your profession and the workplace.
